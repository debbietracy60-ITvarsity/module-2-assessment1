WARTHONGS

The Warthog is a member of the wild pig family, and they are larger than many other species. They have four protrusions that come out of the head and that is where their name comes from. They have small tusks that are sharp and powerful. They use them for digging as well as for fighting.

the common warthog has a huge head,with a mane running down its spine to the middle of the back.it sport sparse hairs on its body and is usually brown and black

the key identifying feature is two pairs of tusk protruding from its mouth that are curved upwards

Where will you find them?

You will find the Warthogs at left side of the Pine city zoo
